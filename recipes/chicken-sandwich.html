Fried Chicken Sandwich

Description

Butter Chicken Description Butter chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a rich and creamy Indian dish that is popular all over the world. It is made with tender pieces of chicken that are marinated in yogurt and spices, then cooked in a flavorful tomato-based sauce. The sauce is enriched with butter and cream, giving it a luxurious and indulgent flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander seeds
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Steps:

  1. Marinate the Chicken:

    In a large bowl, combine the buttermilk, salt, pepper, paprika, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder. Add the chicken pieces and marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to overnight.

  2. Prepare the Breading:

    In a separate bowl, combine the flour, salt, pepper, paprika, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.

  3. Fry the Chicken:

    Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or deep fryer to 350°F (175°C). Remove the chicken pieces from the marinade and dredge them in the flour mixture, shaking off any excess. Carefully place the chicken pieces in the hot oil and fry for 8-10 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through.

  4. Assemble the Sandwiches:

    While the chicken is frying, toast the hamburger buns. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the oil and drain on paper towels. Place a piece of fried chicken on each bun and top with your favorite toppings. Serve immediately.
