Authentic Kacchi Recipes

Description

Kacchi is a popular Bangladeshi dish that is made with beef, goat meat, or chicken. It is a type of curry that is cooked with a variety of spices, including cumin, coriander, turmeric, and chili peppers. Kacchi is typically served with rice or roti.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of beef, goat meat, or chicken, cut into small pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon of coriander seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of garam masala
  • 1 cup of plain yogurt
  • 1 cup of water
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
  1. Marinate the Meat:

    Combine the meat with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Marinate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for maximum flavor.

  2. Cook the Onions and Spices:

    Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add chopped onions and cook until golden brown. Add cumin seeds, coriander seeds, and green chilies. Saut√© for a few seconds until fragrant.

  3. Add the Meat:

    Add the marinated meat to the pot and cook until browned on all sides.

  4. Simmer and Cook:

    Pour in water, bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer, covered, until the meat is tender. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.

  5. Thicken the Gravy:

    If desired, thicken the gravy by adding a slurry of cornstarch and water. Simmer for a few more minutes until the desired consistency is reached.

  6. Serve:

    Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or roti.
