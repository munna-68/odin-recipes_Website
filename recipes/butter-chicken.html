Butter Chicken Recipes

Description

A classic American comfort food, the Fried Chicken Sandwich is a delicious combination of crispy, juicy fried chicken, soft buns, and your favorite toppings. This recipe delivers a homemade version that rivals any fast-food chain.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Your favorite toppings, such as lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and your favorite sauce

Steps

  1. Marinate the Chicken:

    In a bowl, combine yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Add the chicken pieces and marinate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for maximum flavor.

  2. Cook the Onions and Spices:

    Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add chopped onions and cook until golden brown. Add cumin seeds, coriander seeds, and green chilies. Saut√© for a few seconds until fragrant.

  3. Add the Chicken:

    Add the marinated chicken pieces to the pot and cook until browned on all sides.

  4. Simmer and Cook:

    Pour in water, bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer, covered, until the chicken is tender. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.

  5. Make the Butter Sauce:

    In a separate pan, melt butter and add chopped tomatoes. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and the sauce is smooth. Add cream and simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens.

  6. Combine and Finish:

    Pour the butter sauce over the cooked chicken and simmer for a few more minutes until the flavors are well combined. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with naan bread.
